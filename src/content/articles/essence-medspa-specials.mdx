## Why Seasonal Specials Make Strategic Sense for Your Skin

The best medspa specials aren't just about saving money — they're timed around your skin's actual needs. Skin behaves differently across the seasons, and the treatments that produce the best results for dryness and dullness in winter are different from those that address hyperpigmentation and sun protection in summer. Seasonal promotions at Essence Medspa are curated with this in mind.

**Spring** is prime time for resurfacing treatments. After Chicago's long winter, most skin has accumulated a layer of dead cells and dullness. This is when we focus on brightening, exfoliation, and preparing skin for the warmer months.

**Summer** shifts toward maintenance and protection. We feature hydration-forward treatments and emphasize sun damage prevention alongside treatments that keep skin balanced despite heat and humidity.

**Fall** is our most popular season for intensive treatment courses. As UV exposure decreases and skin heals faster, fall is the ideal time to start a series of treatments targeting hyperpigmentation, texture, or volume loss.

**Winter** centers on barrier repair and deep hydration. We offer targeted promotions on treatments that restore what cold air, indoor heating, and wind take away.

Booking during seasonal promotions means you're getting the right treatment at the right time for your skin — and at a better price.

Our membership structure is built around predictability and value. As a member, you receive:

- **Monthly treatment credit** applied automatically — one professional treatment per month, yours to use however you choose
- **Discounted pricing on all additional services** — members pay less on everything beyond their monthly included treatment
- **Priority booking access** — members get first access to our schedule and to promotional events before they open to the general public
- **No commitment minimums** — we don't lock members into contracts that punish them for life changes; our membership is designed to feel like a benefit, not a trap

### Who Benefits Most From Membership

Membership makes the most sense if you're planning to visit at least once per month and know you'll want access to multiple treatment types. For clients who are just starting out and aren't sure how often they'll visit, we recommend starting with a few individual appointments before committing to membership. We want membership to be a clear value decision, not a pressure sale.

## Bundling Treatments for Maximum Value

Even outside of formal promotions, there are smart ways to stretch your investment at Essence Medspa.

**Book a facial add-on when you schedule a massage.** Adding LED light therapy or a scalp treatment to an existing massage booking costs less than booking it as a standalone, and the treatments complement each other well.

**Pair injectables with a facial.** Many clients schedule a Botox or filler appointment followed by a nourishing facial in the same visit. We time the sequence appropriately (injectables first, facial after), and you leave with a complete refresh rather than just one isolated treatment.

**Take advantage of referral incentives.** When you refer a friend who books their first appointment at Essence Medspa, both you and your friend benefit. Ask our front desk about our current referral structure — it's one of the simplest ways to earn credit toward future treatments.
