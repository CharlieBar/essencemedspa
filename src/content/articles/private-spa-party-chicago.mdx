## What Makes a Spa Party Different From Regular Group Bookings

A private spa party at Essence Medspa isn't just a group of people who happen to have appointments on the same day. It's a coordinated, cohesive experience designed around your group — with treatments selected to flow well together, timing structured so everyone finishes around the same time, and an atmosphere that feels celebratory rather than clinical.

When you book a private spa party with us, you're working with our team to curate the experience. We help you:

- Select treatments that work for your group's size, preferences, and budget
- Build a schedule that allows everyone to enjoy their treatments and still have time to connect
- Customize add-ons and enhancements to elevate the experience
- Accommodate different skin types, sensitivities, or preferences within the group

This is a hosted experience, not a self-service booking situation. Our team is present throughout to make sure everything runs smoothly and everyone feels taken care of.

## Treatment Options for Groups

Part of what makes planning a spa party at Essence Medspa enjoyable is the breadth of treatments available for groups. You can mix and match based on what your guests will love most.

### Couples and Group Massage

Our [couples Swedish massage](/services/couples-swedish-massage) is a perennial favorite for spa parties — not just for romantic couples, but for friends who want to experience the same treatment at the same time in a shared space. The Swedish technique uses long, flowing strokes to ease muscle tension and promote full-body relaxation. It's deeply soothing and accessible to virtually everyone, regardless of experience with massage.

For groups who want something with a bit more therapeutic intensity, our [couples deep tissue massage](/services/couples-deep-tissue-massage) targets chronic muscle tension and deeper layers of soft tissue. It's a better fit for guests who carry stress physically — in the neck, shoulders, or lower back — and want to actually resolve that tension rather than just relax.

Both options can be booked for multiple pairs within your group, running simultaneously for maximum efficiency.

### Facial Treatments

Facials are a natural fit for spa parties because the experience is relaxing, the results are visible, and everyone emerges looking genuinely refreshed. Our [custom facial](/services/custom-facial) is our most flexible option — it's tailored to each individual's skin type and concerns during the session, which means guests with very different skin don't need different bookings. One treatment type, fully personalized for each person.

For groups who want a more intensive facial experience, we can incorporate add-ons like LED light therapy, high-frequency treatment for acne-prone skin, or custom masking. The upgrade options are simple and clearly priced so you can plan your group budget accurately.

### Body Treatments

Nothing signals "this is a real spa experience" quite like a body treatment, and our [body scrub](/services/body-scrub) is one of the most satisfying treatments we offer. A full-body exfoliation session sloughs off dead skin cells, improves circulation, and leaves skin incredibly smooth and soft. If your group is celebrating something warm-weather (a bachelorette before a destination wedding, or a summer birthday), a body scrub pairs beautifully with a self-tanner application or simply feels like the ultimate pre-vacation prep.

Body treatments are also a great equalizer in a mixed group — they don't require any specific skin concern or condition, and almost everyone loves the results.

## How to Plan Your Private Spa Party: Step by Step

### Step 1: Reach Out Early

For groups of four or more, we recommend contacting us at least 3–4 weeks in advance, especially on weekends. Private spa parties require coordinated scheduling and room availability. The more lead time you give us, the easier it is to build the experience you want.

Call us at (773) 763-1212 or book through our online system and note in the comments that you're planning a group event. Our team will follow up to discuss the details.

### Step 2: Confirm Your Guest Count and Treatment Preferences

Once you've reached out, we'll ask for a confirmed headcount and a general sense of what treatments interest the group. You don't need a finalized list immediately — we'll guide you through the options. But knowing whether you're a group of four who wants massages or a group of eight who wants a mix of facials and body treatments helps us start building the right schedule.

### Step 3: Discuss Budget and Packages

We offer transparent, straightforward pricing. For private spa parties, we can build custom packages based on your group's preferences and total budget. We'll present clear options — no hidden fees, no surprise add-ons. You'll know exactly what each guest's experience will cost before you confirm.

### Step 4: Prepare Your Guests

Once everything is booked, share a few simple guidelines with your guests:

- Arrive 10–15 minutes before your appointment time to complete any intake paperwork and settle in
- Avoid heavy meals immediately before body or massage treatments
- Remove contact lenses before facial treatments if possible
- Communicate any allergies, sensitivities, or medical conditions when booking

### Step 5: Show Up and Enjoy

The rest is on us. Our team handles the logistics so you can be fully present with your group.

## Practical Tips for an Unforgettable Spa Party

- **Keep the group size manageable.** Four to eight guests is the sweet spot for most spa party experiences. Everyone gets personal attention and the group stays intimate enough to feel cohesive.
- **Stagger arrival times slightly for large groups.** If you have eight or more guests, having everyone arrive at the exact same minute creates a bottleneck. We'll help you plan a staggered arrival that keeps things smooth.
- **Consider a post-treatment gathering.** Whether it's brunch at a nearby Edgebrook spot or wine back at someone's home, plan something after the spa so the energy continues.
- **Communicate dietary restrictions if you plan to bring food or drinks.** We're happy to accommodate small celebrations with champagne, sparkling water, or light snacks — just let us know in advance.
