## 7. Observations

### SERP feature density
- **People Also Ask:** Dense on every informational and comparison query. PAA blocks expand up to 8–10 questions when clicked, making them the single richest source of article-brief fuel for this pillar.
- **Local Pack (Map 3-pack):** Triggers on every "…Chicago" and "near me" query. Currently dominated by Lincoln Park / West Loop / Gold Coast / North Shore medspas because of review volume and NAP density — Essence's Jefferson Park / Edgebrook location requires explicit neighborhood modifiers or tight geo-proximity to surface. GMB optimization and hyper-local content are the highest-leverage levers.
- **Featured Snippets:** Rare on the head term, common on PAA and some informational long-tail (e.g., "how often should you get an anti-aging facial" — paragraph snippet from inspiredbeautymedspa.com or kiwi-spa.com). Capturable with tight 40–60 word answer blocks.
- **Video Carousel:** Present on procedure-level queries (microneedling, RF microneedling, Morpheus8). Dominated by YouTube plastic-surgery channels. Not a near-term priority.
- **Image Pack:** Present on "before and after" queries — RealSelf and clinic galleries win. Essence can enter by publishing labeled before/after galleries with proper alt text and structured data.
- **Shopping / Ads:** 3–4 Google Ads run on commercial-intent queries (microneedling Chicago, Morpheus8 Chicago); organic CTR suppressed above the fold.

### Dominant content format
- **Informational queries** → long-form blog posts (1,500–2,500 words) from mid-sized spa/derm practices with H2-per-question structure and FAQ schema.
- **Commercial queries** → service pages from plastic-surgery practices and chain medspas (NorthShore, SpaDerma, Chapter Aesthetic Studio), with pricing CTAs and before/after modules.
- **"Best of" queries** → editorial listicles (Time Out Chicago, beaire.com's aire-magazine) and aggregators (Yelp top-10, Fresha listings).
- **Transactional queries** → local pack + booking-enabled medspa pages + Fresha / Spafinder / Massage Envy aggregators.

### Content length range of top 10 results
- Head/commercial service pages: 800–1,800 words, heavy with service detail + pricing + FAQ.
- Informational blog posts: 1,500–3,000 words, H2-per-subtopic, clear FAQ schema.
- Editorial listicles: 1,200–2,500 words, 8–12 provider entries with short summaries.
- RealSelf / aggregator pages: thin structured content but heavy UGC — not replicable, worth referencing only.
- Sweet spot for Essence's article briefs: 1,800–2,400 words with a strong FAQ section, local trust signals (NAP, Jefferson Park / Edgebrook references), internal links to service pages, and before/after imagery where compliant.

### Essence's current ranking status
- `essence-medspa.com/services/facial-treatments/signature-clinical-rejuvenating/` (Red Carpet Clinical Facial — Signature Anti-Aging) surfaces on page one of "anti-aging facial Chicago," "best anti-aging facial Chicago," and "anti-aging facial near me Chicago." Strong existing authority on the clinical-facial clusters — defend with internal links from every new anti-aging article.
- `essence-medspa.com/services/facial-treatments/visible-lifting-and-firming-clinical/` ranks on "firming facial Chicago." Protect and expand.
- `essence-medspa.com/services/facial-treatments/powerful-anti-wrinkle-clinical/` ranks on "wrinkle facial Chicago." Protect and expand.
- `essence-medspa.com/services/facial-treatments/hydrofacial/` surfaces on "HydraFacial Edgebrook / Chicago" queries — reinforces neighborhood signal.
- No observed ranking on pure procedure queries ("microneedling Chicago," "RF microneedling Chicago," "Morpheus8 Chicago"). These remain greenfield and are the primary new-content targets for the sub-pillar.
- No observed ranking on Jet Plasma queries — the thinnest, most capturable SERP in this cluster.
