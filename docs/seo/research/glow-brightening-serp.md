## 7. Observations

### SERP feature density
- **Local pack (3-pack)** fires on every "[service] Chicago" and "[service] near me" head + mid-tail query across this sub-pillar. Expect 3-pack to occupy the most visible slot; GBP optimization for Essence (Jefferson Park, 60630, 773-763-1212) is non-negotiable for CTR.
- **People Also Ask** present on roughly 80%+ of sampled SERPs, often 4 items, frequently expanding to 8. PAA is the dominant on-page feature and the biggest addressable opportunity via FAQ schema.
- **Featured snippets**: rare on head/mid-tail transactional Chicago queries, but common on informational long-tail (how often, what is, how long). Most snippets are list or paragraph format — content should be authored in 40–60 word direct-answer blocks.
- **AI Overview / SGE** is increasingly firing on informational queries (vitamin C vs niacinamide, hydrafacial vs oxygen, causes of melasma). Structured Q&A blocks with clear definitions and authoritative citations are being surfaced.
- **Ads**: heavy on hydrafacial Chicago and dark spot treatment Chicago. Organic CTR is compressed here; the informational long-tail and neighborhood-geo long-tail are far cheaper to win.
- **Image pack** fires on "before and after" modifiers. Essence should publish branded before/after imagery with descriptive alt text.
- **Video carousel** fires on "hydrafacial" and "glutathione" queries — YouTube testimonials and explainer Reels/Shorts represent a secondary channel opportunity.

### Dominant content format
- **Service pages** are the primary ranking surface for transactional/commercial head terms — typically 800–1,400 words, benefit-led, with FAQ and pricing or pricing-range language.
- **Condition pages** (hyperpigmentation, melasma, dark spots) win when they stack: what it is → causes → treatment options at this clinic → FAQ → related services. Bella Sana and VITAHL execute this pattern well.
- **Comparison posts** (HydraFacial vs oxygen, DiamondGlow vs HydraFacial, vitamin C vs niacinamide) are dominated by national med/derm blogs rather than local operators — this is a **major gap Essence can own with locally framed comparison content**.
- **Timeline/pre-event posts** (wedding facial, event prep) rank with 1,200–2,000 word structured posts. Underserved locally.

### Content length range
- Service pages: 700–1,500 words (median ~1,000).
- Condition/educational pages: 1,200–2,500 words.
- Comparison blog posts: 1,500–3,000 words with tables.
- Pre-event/timeline posts: 1,500–2,500 words.
- Top-ranking pages almost always include a FAQ block with 6–10 Q&As.

### Essence's current ranking status (visible signals)
- **essence-medspa.com/services/facial-treatments/brightening-vitamin-c/** — ranks top 10 organic for "brightening facial Chicago" and #1 for "vitamin C facial Chicago" (exact-match slug wins). Surfaces for "brightening vitamin C facial Chicago."
- **essence-medspa.com/services/facial-treatments/oxygen-facial/** — ranks top 3 for "oxygen facial Chicago." Very strong.
- **essence-medspa.com/services/med-spa/iv-drips/** — ranks top 5 for "glutathione IV Chicago." Under-optimized for the brightening angle specifically; the page positions drips broadly rather than owning the skin-brightening narrative.
- **No current ranking observed** for: hyperpigmentation Chicago, dark spot treatment Chicago, melasma Chicago, HydraFacial Chicago, DiamondGlow Chicago, vitamin C vs niacinamide, hydrafacial vs oxygen facial.
- **Neighborhood geo whitespace**: "brightening facial Jefferson Park," "medspa 60630," "hydrafacial Jefferson Park," "oxygen facial Edgebrook" — thin SERPs with high conversion intent and virtually no optimized competing pages. This is the cheapest, highest-ROI capture zone for the sub-pillar.

2. Build net-new **condition pillars** for hyperpigmentation, dark spots, melasma, and post-inflammatory marks — each with a "treatment options at Essence" decision block linking to the relevant service page(s).
3. Build net-new **comparison content** (HydraFacial vs oxygen, DiamondGlow vs HydraFacial, vitamin C vs niacinamide, chemical peel vs laser for dark spots) — locally framed, first-party photography, booking CTA.
4. Build an **event-timeline hub** ("facial before a wedding," "pre-event skincare timeline Chicago") to capture bridal and red-carpet intent.
5. Launch **neighborhood geo pages / internal anchors** for Jefferson Park, Edgebrook, Norwood Park, Portage Park, Lincoln Square, Mayfair — all feeding the brightening sub-pillar.
